About These Hadiths

We display Hadiths ONLY from Sahih al-Bukhari and Sahih Muslim, which are universally accepted as the two most authentic Hadith collections in Islam. Both collections have been rigorously verified by Islamic scholars throughout history.

Sahih al-Bukhari: Compiled by Imam Muhammad al-Bukhari (810-870 CE), contains approximately 7,563 Hadiths with repetitions, selected from 600,000 Hadiths.

Sahih Muslim: Compiled by Imam Muslim ibn al-Hajjaj (821-875 CE), contains approximately 12,000 Hadiths (with repetitions) selected from 300,000 Hadiths.
